# Canadian Business Source Notes

This showcase uses generated Office fixtures based on familiar Government of Canada business-startup workflows. It is not official tax, legal, accounting, or grant advice.

## Source Pattern

- Keep official Canada.ca and provincial pages as source references.
- Generate compact fixtures for conversion tests, with clean metadata and no personal records.
- Use mirrors to turn dense government guidance into searchable, linked markdown.
