# Service Readiness Playbook

## Purpose

Prepare each pilot deployment so support, documentation, and escalation paths are ready before the
system ships.

## Checklist

1. Confirm the account success owner and technical escalation owner.
2. Attach the discovery brief and implementation notes to the account hub.
3. Review the latest service readiness deck.
4. Add warranty, support-window, and spare-part assumptions to the relevant account note.
5. Log unresolved decisions in the account hub instead of creating separate one-off notes.
